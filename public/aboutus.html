Who Are We?

We are pair of students who aim to create a website catering the needs of students, employees, and individuals in scheduling and task managing.

About Joie

I am a student who aims to develop a better sense of time management which is why I decided to create this website with my pair. With the responsibilities that I have at home, in school, and in my community, I want to make it easier for me to manage my tasks and deliverables through creating a task list and schedule.

About Erin

Now that we are currently in the “hardest year” of the Pisay life where requirements sometimes pile up, I have a hard time balancing my schedule both inside and outside school. Thus, my pair and I decided to create a task-management website that we hope will help our school community complete tasks on time in a fun and fashionable manner.
